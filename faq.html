Getting Started — 12 articles

How do I start working with Salim-VA?

Start by choosing an hourly or monthly package on the Pricing section, then use the Get In Touch form to schedule a free consultation. I’ll assess your needs and propose a plan.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes — I offer a free 30-minute consultation to scope your needs and confirm the best package or hourly plan.

What information should I prepare for the consultation?

Have a list of tasks, expected hours per week/month, access details (tools/accounts), and any existing processes. This helps me estimate onboarding effort and timelines.

How long does onboarding take?

Onboarding typically takes 2–7 days depending on task complexity and access required. I’ll provide a clear timeline during the consultation.

Which tools do you work with?

I work with common tools like Google Workspace, Microsoft Office, Trello, Asana, Slack, Notion, Canva, and basic e-commerce platforms. If you use a specific tool, tell me during onboarding.

Can I try hourly work before committing to a monthly package?

Yes — hourly work is a good way to test fit before taking a monthly retainer.

How do you bill for time and deliverables?

For hourly work I invoice per hour; for monthly packages, you pay the set monthly fee. Specific billing terms will be provided with each proposal.

Do you offer refunds?

I offer refunds for unused prepaid hours within 7 days of service start, subject to agreement. Contact me to discuss refunds.

Can I change plans later?

Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade plans. Plan changes typically take effect at the next billing cycle.

What if I need extra hours this month?

Extra hours are billed at the hourly rate specified in your plan or at a mutually agreed rate. I’ll notify you beforehand if additional hours are needed.

How do we communicate (phone, email, Slack)?

I use email and optional tools like Slack, Trello or Asana depending on client preference. We’ll agree on the primary channel during onboarding.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes — I sign NDAs where required and take confidentiality seriously. I will not share your data with unauthorized parties.

Account Management — 8 articles

How do I create an account?

There is no formal account sign-up. Use the Get In Touch form or email me directly. I’ll follow up with next steps and any onboarding paperwork.

How do you track hours?

I track hours using a simple time-tracking tool and provide regular summaries. If you prefer a specific time-tracker, we can adopt it.

How will I receive progress updates?

I provide weekly summaries and regular updates for ongoing projects. For urgent items, email or the agreed chat channel works best.

Can I give access to accounts/tools?

Yes — I will request only the access necessary to complete tasks. Where possible, use limited-permission accounts or temporary access and revoke when not required.

Will you work with my team?

Yes — I can coordinate directly with your team members when needed, respecting confidentiality and your communication preferences.

Do you manage client files and documents?

I can manage documents, organize folders, and maintain shared resources in Google Drive or other platforms you use.

How do you handle sensitive credentials?

I recommend using password managers and granting limited access. I never store credentials insecurely; follow best practices during onboarding.

What if I need a temporary surge in support?

For month-to-month clients, it’s usually straightforward to increase hours for a short period. I’ll confirm availability and pricing before accepting extra work.

Billing & Payments — 6 articles

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept common online payment methods as listed on the Pricing/Checkout flow. Payments are processed through secure third-party providers.

When am I billed for monthly packages?

Monthly packages are billed upfront on the agreed recurring date. Yearly plans are billed as a single upfront payment at the discounted rate.

Do unused hours roll over?

By default, unused monthly hours do not roll over. If you expect variable needs, contact me to discuss a custom arrangement.

How are additional hours billed?

Additional hours beyond a package are billed at the agreed hourly rate or a pre-agreed add-on rate. I will notify you before exceeding package hours.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds for unused prepaid hours are available within 7 days of service start, subject to terms agreed at purchase.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes — invoices are provided for all purchases and can be emailed to you for accounting purposes.

Security & Privacy — 5 articles

How do you protect my data?

I use secure storage, limit access to only what’s required for tasks, and follow encryption and password best practices. I also recommend using password managers and temporary access where possible.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. I am happy to sign NDAs when requested to protect sensitive information.

Where is data stored?

Files and data are stored on secure third-party platforms (e.g., Google Drive, secure hosting). I will follow your preference when feasible.

What happens if there is a security incident?

I will notify you promptly, contain the issue, and take steps to remediate. I follow standard incident response best practices.

Can you delete my data on request?

Yes — you can request deletion of your personal data. I will comply with reasonable deletion requests and confirm when the data is removed.
