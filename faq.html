What services do you offer?

We provide a wide range of virtual assistant services including:

  • Administrative support: Email management, calendar scheduling, travel arrangements, document preparation.
  • Graphic design: Logos, social media graphics, slides, marketing assets.
  • Content creation: Blogs, web copy, newsletters, email campaigns.
  • Social media management: Content calendars, post scheduling, engagement, reporting.
  • E-commerce support: Product listings, inventory updates, order handling, customer messaging.
  • Video editing: Short-form edits, captions, YouTube and promotional video edits.
Do you offer dedicated virtual assistants?

Yes, all plans include dedicated assistants who work exclusively on your account.

How quickly can tasks be completed?

Simple tasks: 24–48 hours. Complex tasks: 3–5 business days. Timelines are always provided upfront.

Do you provide training for virtual assistants?

Yes, all assistants undergo technical, communication, and security training. Ongoing training ensures best practices.

How do I get started?

Choose a service package that fits your needs, complete the onboarding process, and your assistant will reach out within 48 hours to start working on your tasks.

What are your pricing plans?

We offer hourly and monthly packages:

  • Hourly: $25–50/hr depending on service type.
  • Monthly Retainers:
    • Starter: $400/month – 20 hours
    • Standard: $750/month – 40 hours
    • Premium: $1,400/month – 80 hours

Yearly plans offer a 20% discount. Custom packages are available for larger requirements.

Can I change my service plan later?

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Mid-cycle upgrades are prorated.

How do you ensure the security of my business data?

All assistants sign NDAs. We use encrypted communication channels, secure file sharing, and follow industry best practices. Systems are regularly audited.

How do you handle billing and payments?

We bill monthly in advance via secure processing. Invoices are emailed. Major credit cards are accepted, and ACH is available for enterprise clients.

What happens to unused hours?

Unused hours roll over for up to 60 days. After that, they expire to maintain fairness and availability.

Still have questions? Contact our support team for assistance.
