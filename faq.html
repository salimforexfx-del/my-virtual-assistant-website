What services do your virtual assistants provide?

Administrative support, calendar & email management, content creation, social media management, data entry and research, customer service support, and more. Everything is tailored to your business needs.

How do I get started with Salim-VA?

Choose an hourly or monthly package in Pricing, then book a free consultation. We'll match you with a suitable assistant and set up tools & communication preferences.

What are your pricing plans?

We offer hourly services and monthly packages (Starter, Standard, Premium). Monthly plans provide predictable hours and cost savings; yearly billed upfront saves 20%.

How do you ensure security of my data?

We use encryption, secure cloud storage, role-based access, and NDAs. Assistants only access what’s necessary for task completion and receive regular security training.

Can I change my service plan later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes typically take effect from the next billing cycle. Contact Support to coordinate migration and scheduling.

What if I'm not satisfied with my virtual assistant?

If you’re not satisfied, we’ll reassign you to a different assistant and aim to resolve the issue quickly. Refunds for unused hours are available within 7 days subject to our policy.

How quickly can tasks be completed?

Turnaround depends on complexity. Routine tasks are often completed within 24–48 hours. We provide estimated timelines for larger projects during onboarding.

Do you provide training for virtual assistants?

Yes, our assistants receive ongoing training in tools, communication, and security to ensure consistent high-quality delivery.
