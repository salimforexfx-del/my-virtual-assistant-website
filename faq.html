What services do your virtual assistants provide?

Our assistants provide administrative support, calendar & email management, content creation, social media management, data entry and research, customer service support, and more. Services are tailored to your specific business needs.

How do I get started with Salim-VA?

Choose an hourly or monthly package from the Pricing section, then book a free consultation. We'll match you with the best assistant, review your requirements, and set up communication & tools.

What are your pricing plans?

We offer flexible hourly rates and monthly packages (Starter, Standard, Premium). Monthly plans include set hours per month and often a discount versus hourly billing. Visit the Pricing page for details.

How do you ensure the security of my business data?

We use industry-standard encryption, secure cloud storage, role-based access, and all assistants sign NDAs. We also conduct security training and limit data access to only what’s necessary for task completion.

Can I change my service plan later?

Yes—you can upgrade or downgrade your plan. Plan changes typically take effect from the next billing cycle. Contact support for assistance with migration.

What if I'm not satisfied with my virtual assistant?

If you’re not satisfied, we’ll work to reassign you to a different assistant and address any concerns. We also offer refunds for unused hours within 7 days of service start, subject to our refund policy.

How do you handle billing and payments?

Payments are processed securely through third-party payment providers. Invoices are issued for packages and additional services, and payment terms are specified at purchase.

What time zones do your assistants work in?

Our assistants operate across multiple time zones and can align with your preferred working hours for seamless collaboration.

Do you offer dedicated virtual assistants?

Yes—depending on your plan you may opt for a dedicated assistant who focuses only on your account and tasks.

What happens to unused hours?

For monthly packages, unused hours typically do not roll over. We’ll work with you to ensure hours are used efficiently; special arrangements may be possible on a case-by-case basis.

How quickly can tasks be completed?

Completion time depends on task complexity. Many routine tasks are completed within 24–48 hours; complex projects may take longer and will have estimated timelines provided during onboarding.

Do you provide training for virtual assistants?

Yes. Assistants receive ongoing training in tools, client communication, and data handling to ensure high standards and up-to-date practices.
