Frequently Asked Questions

Find clear answers about onboarding, packages, billing, security, and how I work. Click a category tab to filter to the questions you care about.

Getting Started

How do I start working with Salim-VA?

Choose an hourly or monthly package from the Pricing page, or contact me via the Get In Touch form. I’ll follow up with a short consultation to scope your needs and confirm next steps.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes — I offer a free 30-minute consultation to understand your needs and recommend the best plan or approach.

What should I prepare for onboarding?

Prepare a list of tasks, desired hours, access to necessary tools/accounts, any templates or brand guidelines, and preferred communication channels.

How long does onboarding take?

Onboarding typically takes 2–7 days depending on complexity and access required. I’ll give a clear timeline during the consultation.

Can I start with hourly work instead of a monthly package?

Yes — hourly work is a great way to try my services before committing to a retainer.

Which tools do you use?

I commonly use Google Workspace, Trello/Asana, Slack, Notion, Canva, and basic e-commerce platforms. I’ll adapt to your preferred tools where possible.

How soon can you start?

Start dates depend on current availability. In many cases I can start within a few days; I’ll confirm specific dates during our consultation.

Do you provide a written onboarding plan?

Yes — I provide an onboarding checklist and timeline so you know what to expect and which access/credentials to prepare.

Can I book a call directly?

Yes — use the Get In Touch form or the Support page to schedule a call; I’ll follow up with available time slots.

What industries do you work with?

I support small businesses, founders, creators, e-commerce sellers, and agencies. My skills are broadly applicable; if you have a niche need, ask and I’ll let you know if I’m a fit.

Do you offer package customizations?

Yes — packages can be tailored where needed. If your needs don’t fit a standard plan, I’ll propose a custom retainer.

What time zone do you operate in?

I’m based in Nairobi (EAT), and I adapt hours to match client needs — especially U.S. time zones — where possible.

Account Management

How will you track hours?

I use a reliable time-tracker and provide weekly summaries. If you prefer a specific tracker, I can adopt it for your account.

How do you report progress?

Clients receive weekly updates and progress reports. For ad-hoc tasks I’ll provide completion notes as requested.

Can you work with my team?

Yes — I coordinate with your team members while maintaining confidentiality and following your communication style.

How do you handle file access?

I request only the minimum access necessary; use limited-permission accounts or temporary access when possible.

Do you organize documents?

Yes — I can structure folders, standardize naming, and keep shared drives tidy for easy handoff and reference.

How are credentials stored?

I recommend using password managers and temporary logins. I never store sensitive credentials insecurely.

Can I change account owners or contacts?

Yes — provide written authorization and I’ll update contact details and permissions as needed.

What happens at contract end?

On termination I return or securely delete files per your preference and provide a handover summary of open tasks.

Billing & Payments

What payment methods are accepted?

Payments are processed via secure third-party providers. Exact payment methods are presented during checkout (USD).

When am I billed for monthly packages?

Monthly packages are billed upfront on your billing date. Yearly plans are billed as a single upfront payment at the discounted rate.

Do unused hours roll over?

By default unused monthly hours do not roll over. Custom arrangements can be discussed if you need flexible handling.

How are additional hours billed?

Extra hours are billed at the hourly rate stated in your plan or at an agreed add-on rate. I will ask for approval before exceeding package limits.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds for unused prepaid hours are available within 7 days of service start, subject to the agreement provided at purchase.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes — invoices are provided for all purchases and can be sent via email for accounting and records.

Security & Privacy

How do you protect my data?

I use secure storage, limited access, encryption where appropriate, and best practices for credentials. I recommend password managers for client credentials.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes — I will sign NDAs on request to protect sensitive information.

Where is data stored?

Files are stored on secure third-party platforms (Google Drive, secure hosting). I follow your preferences where feasible.

What if there is a security incident?

I will notify you promptly, contain the issue, and remediate. I follow standard incident response practices to limit impact.

Can you delete my data on request?

Yes — you can request deletion or export of your data. I’ll confirm once the action has been completed.

Services

What services do you offer?

I provide administrative support, graphic design, content creation, social media management, e-commerce support, and video editing — all delivered by me directly as Salim-VA.

Can you create social media content?

Yes — I create post graphics, captions, schedules, and can help with engagement and reporting depending on your package.

Do you provide dedicated VA services?

My retainers provide a predictable allocation of hours per month; for dedicated or full-time coverage we discuss availability and custom pricing.

How do you price projects?

Pricing is hourly or monthly retainer-based. For projects I may propose a fixed fee depending on scope.

Can you help scale operations?

Yes — I help document processes, manage workflows, and put routine tasks on autopilot so you can scale more easily.

What languages do you support?

I work primarily in English. For other languages, contact me to confirm capability for your project.

Still have questions?

Contact me directly for quick answers or schedule a free consultation.
