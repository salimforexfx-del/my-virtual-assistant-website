Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our virtual assistant services and support.

What services do you offer?

We provide a wide range of virtual assistant services including:

  • Administrative support: Email management, calendar scheduling, travel arrangements, document preparation.
  • Graphic design: Logos, social media graphics, slides, marketing assets.
  • Content creation: Blogs, web copy, newsletters, email campaigns.
  • Social media management: Content calendars, post scheduling, engagement, reporting.
  • E-commerce support: Product listings, inventory updates, order handling, customer messaging.
  • Video editing: Short-form edits, captions, YouTube and promotional video edits.
How do I get started?

Getting started is simple! Choose a service package that fits your needs, complete the onboarding process, and your assistant will reach out within 48 hours to discuss your requirements and start working on your tasks.

What are your pricing plans?

We offer hourly and monthly packages:

  • Hourly: $25–50/hr depending on service type.
  • Monthly Retainers:
    • Starter: $400/month – 20 hours
    • Standard: $750/month – 40 hours
    • Premium: $1,400/month – 80 hours

Yearly plans offer a 20% discount. Custom packages are available for large-scale requirements.

Can I change my service plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Mid-cycle upgrades are prorated so you can start using additional hours immediately.

How do you ensure the security of my business data?

All assistants sign NDAs, and we use encrypted communication channels, secure file sharing, and follow industry best practices. Our systems are regularly audited to ensure compliance.

How do you handle billing and payments?

We bill monthly in advance via secure processing. Invoices are emailed, and payment methods can be updated anytime. Major credit cards are accepted, and ACH is available for enterprise clients.

What happens to unused hours?

Unused hours roll over for up to 60 days, allowing flexibility. After 60 days, unused hours expire to ensure fairness and availability.

Do you offer dedicated virtual assistants?

Yes, all plans include dedicated assistants who work exclusively on your account, ensuring consistency and a strong working relationship.

How quickly can tasks be completed?

Simple tasks typically complete within 24–48 hours, while complex projects may take 3–5 business days. Estimated timelines are always provided upfront.

Do you provide training for virtual assistants?

Yes. All assistants undergo comprehensive training in technical skills, communication, security protocols, and industry knowledge. Ongoing training ensures best practices are followed.

Still have questions? Contact our support team for assistance.
