Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our virtual assistant services.

What services do your virtual assistants provide?

Our assistants handle administrative support, content creation, social media management, customer service, research, and more. Services are customized to your needs.

How do I get started with Salim-VA?

You can start by choosing an hourly or monthly package on our website, then book a consultation to outline your requirements.

What are your pricing plans?

We offer flexible hourly services and monthly packages. Visit our Pricing section for details.

How do you ensure the security of my business data?

We use industry-standard encryption, secure data centers, and all assistants sign NDAs. Confidentiality and security are top priorities.

Can I change my service plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle.

What if I'm not satisfied with my virtual assistant?

If you're not fully satisfied, we can reassign you to a new assistant. We also offer refunds for unused hours within 7 days of service start.

How do you handle billing and payments?

Payments are processed securely via Paystack. Fees are charged upfront for packages, with clear invoices provided.

What time zones do your assistants work in?

Our assistants are flexible and can adapt to your preferred time zone for seamless collaboration.

Do you offer dedicated virtual assistants?

Yes. Depending on your package, you can have a dedicated assistant working exclusively with your business.

What happens to unused hours?

For monthly packages, unused hours do not roll over but we’ll help you maximize usage. Hourly services are billed per use.

How quickly can tasks be completed?

Turnaround depends on the complexity of the task. Most tasks are completed within 24–48 hours.

Do you provide training for virtual assistants?

Yes, we ensure all assistants receive ongoing training in tools, communication, and client service to stay updated.
