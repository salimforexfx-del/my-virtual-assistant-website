Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At Salim-VA, your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights in relation to that data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email address, phone number, company name when you contact us or sign up for services.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages.
  • Communication Data: records of emails, chat messages and support tickets exchanged with our team.
  • Payment Information: billing details and payment method information (processed securely via third-party providers).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate and improve our virtual assistant services.
  • To communicate with you about services, updates, and support.
  • To process payments and manage billing.
  • To analyze site usage and improve user experience.
  • To send marketing materials where you have provided consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Information Sharing

We share data only as necessary with trusted third parties who help operate our business (payment processors, cloud storage, analytics), with assigned virtual assistants to complete your tasks, or when required by law. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest, maintain strict access controls, conduct regular security audits, and provide employee training on data protection. Despite this, no system can be 100% secure; however, we follow best practices to minimize risk.

Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Portability of your data
  • Restriction of processing or opt-out of marketing

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for site functionality, analytics cookies to understand usage, and optional marketing cookies with consent. Manage cookie preferences through your browser settings; note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.
