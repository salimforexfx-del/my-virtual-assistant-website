Privacy Policy

How I protect and handle your information

My Commitment to Your Privacy

Hi, I'm Salim. Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit my website or use my services. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access the site.

Information I Collect

I collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to me when you express interest in my services, when you fill out the contact form, or when you communicate with me directly.

Personal Information

The personal information I collect may include:

  • Your name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Your company name and business details
  • Payment information (processed securely through Paystack or PayPal - I never see your full card details)
  • Any information you share in your messages to me

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit my website, I automatically collect certain information through cookies and analytics, including:

  • Your IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages you visit and time spent
  • How you found my website

This helps me understand how people use my site so I can improve it.

How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect to:

  • Provide you with the virtual assistant services you request
  • Process your payments and send you receipts
  • Respond to your questions, comments, and requests
  • Send you updates about your projects
  • Improve my website and services based on how people use them
  • Protect against fraud or unauthorized transactions

Sharing Your Information

I take your privacy seriously and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone. I only share information with:

  • Payment Processors: Paystack and PayPal process your payments securely. They have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect my legal rights, I may disclose information.

Data Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. I use secure connections and never store sensitive payment information on my servers.

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • You can request access to the information I hold about you
  • You can ask me to correct inaccurate information
  • You can request deletion of your information (though I may need to keep some records for legal purposes)
  • You can opt out of marketing communications at any time

Just email me and I'll help with any of these requests.

Cookies

My website uses cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but some parts of the site may not function properly.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy occasionally. When I do, I'll update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom. I encourage you to review it periodically.
