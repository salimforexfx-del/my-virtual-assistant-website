Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024 — how Salim-VA collects, uses and protects your information.

Salim-VA is operated by me, Salim. I take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information I collect, why I collect it, and how I protect it when you use my services or contact me.

Information I collect

  • Personal information: name, email address, phone number, company name when you contact me or sign up for services.
  • Usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site to improve the experience.
  • Communication records: emails, support messages and any materials you share to help me complete your work.
  • Payment data: billing details processed securely through third-party payment providers when you purchase services.

How I use your information

  • To provide and improve Salim-VA services and communicate with you about them.
  • To process payments and manage billing through secure third-party processors.
  • To analyze website usage so I can improve the site and service offering.
  • To send occasional marketing communications only when you have consented.
  • To comply with legal obligations when required.

Information sharing

I only share data when necessary — for example, with trusted third-party service providers (payment processors, hosting, analytics) who help run the service, or if required by law. I do not sell your personal information.

Data security

I take reasonable measures to protect data, including secure storage, encryption where appropriate, and access controls. While I work to keep data safe, no system is completely foolproof; I follow best practices to minimize risk.

Your rights

You can request access to your personal data, ask for corrections, request deletion, request portability, or opt out of marketing communications. Contact me to exercise any of these rights (see contact details below).

Cookies & tracking

Cookies are used for site functionality and analytics. Marketing cookies are used only with consent. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser; disabling cookies may affect site functionality.
